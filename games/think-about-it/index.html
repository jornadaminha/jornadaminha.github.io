Think About It inspired by Jackbox Games

Think About It is a party game about creating and destroying conspiracies.

In Round 1 ("Found Evidence") players are given two completely unrelated topics and must connect them into a convincing (or completely ridiculous) conspiracy theory.
After a short note-writing phase, players present their findings using randomly assigned "evidence" images and answer follow-up questions from the audience.
Conspiracies are then judged on two important factors: how funny they are, and whether they could possibly be true.

In Round 2 ("You're Wrong!") the theories begin to fall apart. Each conspiracy is assigned to another player, who must prove it wrong before presenting an even better explanation of their own.

At the end of the game, a final awards show celebrates the funniest theories, the most believable theories, and the overall winner.
